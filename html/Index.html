Want to know more?

Cold Storage is a Singaporean multinational supermarket company currently owned by DFI Retail Group. Its parent company also operates Market Place stores, now branded as Cold Storage Fresh, as well as the Giant hypermarket brand, and has various other supermarkets around Asia including Hero and Wellcome.

Born in Singapore in 1903, we started out as a homegrown merchant of fresh, quality meats and dairy products. From a small cold store to a popular household name and Singaporean icon, we introduced numerous innovative retail concepts including the first supermarket in the 1960s, multiple retailing and bulk buying to bring greater savings to our customers. Cold Storage is also a pioneer in bringing quality and exclusive products from around the world to the well-travelled and the residents of Singapore.
